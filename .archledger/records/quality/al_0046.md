## Quality Requirement

Every compiled note must maintain accurate `source_count` parity with its `sources` frontmatter list.

### Acceptance Criteria

- `source_count` field value equals `len(sources)` for every compiled note.
- `sync_source_count()` in `build.py` detects and fixes mismatches.
- `source_coverage_report()` maps sources to compiled notes bidirectionally.

### Implementation

- `note_normalize()` in `notes.py` checks and optionally fixes source_count.
- `sync_source_count()` runs as part of `build_vault()`.
- Coverage gaps reported by `source_delta` as `missing_coverage` category.
